Zero Day Exploit is built to make hacking feel cinematic and meaningful.

The game blends cyberpunk aesthetics with a satirical lens on surveillance, power, and digital rebellion. Every story beat is designed to feel alive, reactive and charged.

What this project stands for

Fiction first

All systems, characters, and scenarios are created for narrative impact, not real-world instruction.

Creative independence

The project is community-supported and remains rooted in open development.

Premium style

High-quality UI, immersive text, and polished design create a premium hacker world.

Design pillars

  • Rich atmosphere with neon, static, glow and motion.
  • Player choice that changes faction flows and narrative paths.
  • Meaningful encounters that reward smart play and exploration.
  • Open development philosophy with community input on every update.
