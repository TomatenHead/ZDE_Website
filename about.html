About the Game

The story, vision, and ideals behind Zero Day Exploit.

What is Zero Day Exploit?

Zero Day Exploit is a futuristic, satirical hacking game blending cyberpunk atmosphere, comedy, and narrative choices. It plays with realism while criticizing modern digital culture — without taking itself too seriously.

Core Themes

  • Story: A dark yet humorous tale inside a dystopian digital world.
  • Satire: A comedic reflection on hacking culture, surveillance, capitalism and power.
  • Cyber Atmosphere: Terminals, exploits, networks, encrypted secrets and digital chaos.
  • Player Choice: Every action affects your path through the system.

Ideals & Values (Satirical & Fictional)

  • Anti‑Capitalist: Critiques corporate greed, surveillance capitalism and digital exploitation.
  • Anti‑Authoritarian: Challenges unregulated power, control structures and oppressive systems.
  • Pro‑Piracy: Celebrates digital freedom, open knowledge and resistance against monopolies.
  • Legal notice: We encourage nobody to engage in illegal activities. ( But we can't do anything against it if you do. )
